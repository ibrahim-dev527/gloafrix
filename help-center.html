üõí Buying & Orders

How do I place an order? ‚ñº
To place an order: 1) Browse products and click "Add to Cart", 2) Review your cart and click "Proceed to Checkout", 3) Enter your shipping address and select shipping method, 4) Choose payment method and complete payment, 5) You'll receive an order confirmation email with tracking information.
Can I modify or cancel my order? ‚ñº
You can modify or cancel orders before they're shipped. Go to "My Orders" in your account, find the order, and click "Request Cancellation" or "Modify Order". Once shipped, cancellation isn't possible, but you can return the item after receiving it according to our return policy.
How do I track my order? ‚ñº
Track your order by: 1) Logging into your account, 2) Going to "My Orders", 3) Clicking on the order number, 4) Viewing real-time tracking updates. You'll also receive SMS and email notifications at each delivery milestone.
What if my order is late? ‚ñº
If your order hasn't arrived by the estimated delivery date: 1) Check tracking information for updates, 2) Contact the vendor through the order page, 3) If unresponsive, open a support ticket and we'll investigate. Orders more than 3 days late are eligible for full refunds.
How do returns and refunds work? ‚ñº
Returns: 1) Request return within 7 days of delivery, 2) Item must be unused in original packaging, 3) Ship back using provided label or courier, 4) Refund processed once vendor receives item. Refunds typically take 5-10 business days to reflect in your account.

üí∞ Selling on GloAfrix

How do I become a vendor? ‚ñº
To become a vendor: 1) Click "Sell on GloAfrix" in the footer, 2) Complete vendor registration with business details, 3) Submit required documents (business registration, ID verification), 4) Wait for approval (usually 2-3 business days), 5) Once approved, set up your store and start listing products.
What are the selling fees? ‚ñº
GloAfrix charges: 1) 10% commission on each sale, 2) Standard payment processing fees (2.5% for cards), 3) No monthly subscription or listing fees, 4) Optional premium features available. You only pay when you make a sale!
When and how do I get paid? ‚ñº
Payments are released 7 days after successful delivery. You can withdraw funds to your bank account anytime (minimum GHS 50). Withdrawals typically process within 1-3 business days. View your earnings dashboard to track sales and pending payments.
How do I handle shipping? ‚ñº
You have two options: 1) Use our logistics partners - print labels from your dashboard and drop off at collection points, or 2) Use your own courier - upload tracking information manually. Set realistic delivery times and pack items securely to avoid damage.
What products are prohibited? ‚ñº
You cannot sell: illegal drugs, weapons, counterfeit goods, stolen items, adult content, live animals (except approved partners), hazardous materials, or anything that promotes hate. Full list in our Terms & Conditions. Violations result in account suspension.

üí≥ Payments & Billing

What payment methods are accepted? ‚ñº
We accept: Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard), Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), Bank Transfers, and Cash on Delivery (selected areas). All payments are processed securely through PCI-compliant gateways.
Is my payment information secure? ‚ñº
Yes! We use bank-level encryption (SSL/TLS) for all transactions. We never store complete credit card information. Payment data is processed by certified payment providers (Paystack, Flutterwave) meeting PCI-DSS standards.
Why was my payment declined? ‚ñº
Common reasons: Insufficient funds, incorrect card details, expired card, bank security restrictions, or daily transaction limits. Try: Re-entering card information, using a different payment method, contacting your bank, or waiting 24 hours and trying again.
How do refunds work? ‚ñº
Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days. Refunds go to original payment method: Cards (5-10 days), Mobile Money (1-3 days), Bank Transfer (3-5 days). You'll receive email confirmation when refund is initiated and completed.

üíº Freelancing Services

How do I become a freelancer? ‚ñº
Apply through "Become a Freelancer" page. Submit: portfolio, skills, experience, and hourly rate. Our team reviews applications within 24-48 hours. Once approved, complete your profile and start bidding on projects. Maintain 4+ star rating and 80%+ completion rate.
What are the freelancer fees? ‚ñº
We charge 15% commission on completed projects. First 5 projects have 20% commission. No monthly fees or bidding costs. You keep 85% of your earnings after successful project completion and client approval.
How do project payments work? ‚ñº
Client funds are held in escrow when project starts. Upon completion, submit deliverables for client review. Once approved, payment is released to your account. You can withdraw anytime (minimum GHS 50). Withdrawals process in 1-3 business days.

üë§ Account Settings

How do I reset my password? ‚ñº
Click "Forgot Password" on login page, enter your email, check inbox for reset link (check spam folder), click link and create new password. Link expires in 24 hours. If you don't receive email, contact support@gloafrix.com.
Can I delete my account? ‚ñº
Yes. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Complete pending orders first. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Some information may be retained for legal purposes (transaction history, tax records). Allow 30 days for complete deletion.

üöö Shipping & Delivery

How long does delivery take? ‚ñº
Delivery times vary by location and vendor: Same city (1-3 days), Within Ghana (3-7 days), Cross-border (7-14 days). Estimated delivery date shown at checkout. Track your order in real-time through your account dashboard.
What are the shipping costs? ‚ñº
Shipping costs depend on: Item size/weight, delivery distance, and shipping method. Costs displayed at checkout before payment. Many vendors offer free shipping on orders above certain amounts. Look for "Free Shipping" badges on product listings.
What if my package is damaged? ‚ñº
Report damaged items within 48 hours: Take photos of packaging and product, go to order details and click "Report Issue", upload photos and description. We'll review and arrange replacement or full refund. Don't discard packaging until issue is resolved.
